Chinese Chess (Xiangqi)

Welcome to Chinese Chess

Chinese Chess, also known as Xiangqi, is a two-player strategy board game that is similar to Western Chess. It is played on a board with 9 rows and 10 columns. The objective of the game is to checkmate your opponent's General piece.

On this website, you will find information on the history of Chinese Chess, how to play the game, tips and strategies for winning, and reviews from other players. Join our newsletter to stay up-to-date on the latest Chinese Chess news and updates.
